Frequently Asked Questions

How does telehealth work for this treatment?
Our telehealth platform connects you with licensed physicians through secure video calls. You'll receive the same quality of care as an in-person visit, with the convenience of accessing treatment from home.
Is this treatment covered by insurance?
Many insurance plans cover telehealth consultations and FDA-approved medications. Our team will work with your insurance provider and offer assistance with prior authorizations when needed.
How quickly can I start treatment?
Most patients can start treatment within 24-48 hours after their initial consultation. We'll coordinate medication delivery and provide all necessary supplies directly to your door.
