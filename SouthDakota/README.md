# SouthDakotaDoc - South South Dakota's premier Telehealth Platform

## Overview

SouthDakotaDoc provides discreet, professional telehealth services for Dakotans statewide. Licensed physicians offering weight loss treatments (Semaglutide, Tirzepatide), ED treatment, hair restoration, mental health support, and comprehensive primary care.

- **18 Treatment Services** - Weight loss, ED, hair restoration, mental health, and more

- **State-Licensed Physicians** - All doctors licensed in the state
- **Secure & HIPAA Compliant** - 256-bit encryption, full privacy protection
- **Mobile Optimized** - Perfect experience on all devices
